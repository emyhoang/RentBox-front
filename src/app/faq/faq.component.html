Changing My Order
  • How do I make changes to my order?

If we haven't started packing your order, you can change your rental begin date, change sizes, add an accessory and/or update your shipping address.* Unfortunately, we can't make any changes to orders that are already in the process of being packed (or have already shipped from our warehouse). Once your item ships, you can follow your package in real time here. And don't forget to check out articles on The Shift for expert advice on how to style and accessorize your look. It's extremely important you try everything on the moment it arrives. On the off chance you don't love your item, we can overnight you a replacement style. That's our 100% Guarantee. Learn more about replacements here. The best way to manage your order is with our iPhone app (you can download it here). *Orders typically get packed a few days before your rental begin date.

    It's easy! To find your order number, just navigate to your order history on our website. You can also find it in the confirmation email you received after placing your order.

Recieving My Order
  • What if the dresses I ordered don't fit?
    If this happens, we can help. Choose one of the following options: Option 1: We'll help you find another dress If you reach out to us by 6pm EST (3pm EST if within NYC), we can overnight you another dress in time for you event for $12.95. You can call, email, and chat us for assistance or if you live near one of our stores, you can swap your original dress for another style (no appointment necessary). If the style you select is more expensive, you will be charged the difference in price. If the style is less expensive, you will be given a merchandise credit for the difference in price. Option 2: We'll give you a credit​ We’re happy to give you a Rent the Runway credit (less the cost of shipping, accessories and saleable items) if none of the dresses in your order fit. To receive a credit, go to the “Orders” section of your Rent the Runway account and fill out our Return for Fit Request form. Use the provided packaging (which includes a pre-paid label) and return everything to us within 24 hours of receiving it. Credits do not expire, but we do not issue partial credits or credits for accessories or intimates.
  • My rental hasn't arrived yet. When can I expect to receive it?
    Orders typically ship out 1-2 days before your rental begin date. Your order will be delivered by 8pm EST on the first day of your rental period (NYC residents, it'll get to you before 10pm EST). Don't forget, if you're having your rental delivered to an office or apartment building you'll need someone to sign for the package. You can follow your package's journey here (tracking information will populate once your order is shipped from our fulfillment center).
  • I've placed my order. What do I do next?
    First thing's first, get excited. You're going to look great! Your order will ship out from our fulfillment center 1-2 days before your rental begin date. Once it's shipped, you can track your order in real time. Be sure to try everything on the moment your order arrives. On the off chance you're not totally satisfied with your Reserve rental, we'll do everything in our power to get a replacement style to you in time for your event. That's our 100% Guarantee.
  • When will my order arrive? What will it look like?
    Your order will be delivered to the shipping address you provided by 8pm on the first day of your rental period (NYC residents, it'll get to you before 10pm EST that day). Dresses and clothing will arrive in a navy blue Rent the Runway garment bag. Accessories may be delivered in a separate Rent the Runway box or bag. We do our best to ensure your dresses arrive wrinkle-free. Each dress is steamed and pressed before being sealed in plastic and placed in the garment bag.
Returning My Order
  • What if I lose the UPS return envelope sent with my order?
    No problem, just send your order via UPS to the address below: Rent the Runway Order # 100 Metro Way Secaucus, NJ 07094 Please email a stylist and provide us with your new return tracking information. You will responsible for your own return method.
  • How do I know my order has been successfully returned?
    You'll hear from us! We'll send you an email when we receive your order back at our warehouse (typically 2-3 days after you drop it off at UPS). You can also check the status of your return by logging into your Rent the Runway account.
  • Can I return my rental to a Rent the Runway store?
    Of course! Just make sure you check the store's hours before you head over.
